About AeroPal

AeroPal aims at providing well-routed maps with proper steps to users based
on his/her flight details and the airport, to help users have a smooth transitioning experience inside airports

Future Goals

We plan to further modify the website make it responsive and user friendly.
We also intend to add multiple new features like more detailed maps using dynamic routing.
A mobile-application shall also be developed, to make it more
accessible, speedy, compatible and customizable.
